Claude Community Build Day

Global Brand Studio joined the Claude Community Build Day to design the visuals that welcomed builders into Anthropic’s world. We produced a poster toolkit, motion loops, and a social launch kit that the community team could release quickly.

The system uses soft gradients, bold type, and modular layouts that adapt from print to screens—keeping Claude’s voice present without feeling overly corporate.

Deliverables

Poster series, motion loops, social graphics, on-site signage templates.

Project Role

Claude Community Partners — visual identity, art direction, and rollout support.

Tools

Figma, After Effects, Notion launch checklist.
