About Global Brand Studio

Global Brand Studio is a student-led team at Minerva University. We design brand identities, event worlds, and storytelling tools for partners who want to connect with people in a thoughtful way.

Every project starts with research: who the audience is, how they gather, and what emotions they should feel. From there, we create clear visual systems and practical assets that teams can launch right away.
